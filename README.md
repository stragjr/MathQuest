# MathQuest

## Overview
MathQuest is an engaging and educational game designed to enhance your mathematics skills through fun challenges and puzzles.

## Features
- **Multiple Levels:** Progress through various levels of difficulty.
- **Timed Challenges:** Race against the clock to solve problems.
- **Leaderboard:** Compete with friends and other players worldwide.
- **Hints and Power-ups:** Get assistance when you get stuck.

## How to Play
1. **Start the Game:** Launch MathQuest from your device.
2. **Choose a Level:** Select your desired difficulty level.
3. **Solve Problems:** Answer the math questions presented to you.
4. **Advance Through Levels:** Successfully answering questions will unlock higher difficulty levels.
5. **Track Your Progress:** Check your scores and see how you rank against other players on the leaderboard.

## Installation Instructions
1. **Download the Game:** Visit [MathQuest GitHub Releases](https://github.com/stragjr/MathQuest/releases) to download the latest version.
2. **Install the Application:** Follow the installation instructions specific to your operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux).
3. **Launch MathQuest:** Once installed, open the application and start your mathematical journey!
